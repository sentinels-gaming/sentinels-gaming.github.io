About Sentinels Gaming

Sentinels Gaming is a Multi-Game Gaming Community whose primary mission is making a welcoming and inclusive place for all to call home. Founded by Arcadius Sentinels Gaming has grown exponentially.

About Sentinels Gaming

In 2020 and beyond, Sentinels Gaming is looking to expand our community and improve what we offer. Join us and find your forever home!

Sentinels Gaming loves our events! Not only do we do Fashion Wars, but we also host Weekly Raiding, Fractals and Dungeon Crawls in addition to more relaxing Events like our Nekkid Scurry, Funtivities and Guild Hall Shenanigans!

About Pewpew

HIYOS! My name is Pewpew, and I am the original Founder/Leader of The TINY Army. I came up with the idea for an Asura takeover one day and made it happen just for funzies. However, after much talking about it with my close friend, Tiny Taimi, we decided to make the guild happen, and what started as a simple joke, Pewpews Tiny Army [TINY] became the #1 hangout guild for everything Asura! Eventual we grew into “The TINY Army” with 3 active guilds, and even though I take the reins for most major guild related events and communication, I could not do it without the help of Co-Leaders Taimi, Samayou, Plorrt, and Gripph, as well as my other amazing guild Officers. I may not say it out loud, but as a person who is typically quiet and keeps to myself, I am proud to be a part of something so smol and big at the same time, and to have made hundreds of friends in the process. Here’s to you, TINY Family!

-Pewpew Kapew

Join TINY

Ready to join the TINY Army? Great! We currently run three guilds, Pewpew’s TINY Army, Taimi’s TINY Army, and Samayou’s TINY Army. No matter which guild you join, we operate by the same rules, have the same officers, and participate in the same events. Please do not fill out this form if you have already been invited into the guild. Please allow up to 3 days for your request to be processed (NOTE: This application is NOT for the website. It is to join the in-game guilds) .

Pewpew’s TINY Army: Usually full; May have a waiting list, if you want to join this guild, There may be an extra wait. Members active up to one month.

Taimi’s TINY Army: Taking applications, no waiting list. Members active up to one month.

Samayou’s TINY Army: Taking applications, no waiting list. Members active up to two months.
